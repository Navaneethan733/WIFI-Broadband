Our Story

Connecting communities
since 2008

STACKLY began with a simple mission: to provide ultra-fast, reliable internet to homes and businesses that were consistently let down by legacy providers. Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small neighborhood startup into a nationwide fiber-optic powerhouse.

We believe that internet access is a fundamental necessity in the modern framework. Our continuous investment in 10Gbps core infrastructure and cutting-edge routing technologies ensures that our customers never experience buffering, packet loss, or downtime. We don't just provide an internet connection; we provide seamless digital experiences.

Get In Touch

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide by deploying blazing-fast fiber internet and delivering unparalleled customer service. We strive to empower small businesses and families alike by providing the digital foundation they need to succeed in a hyper-connected world.

Our Vision

To become the most universally trusted internet service provider on the continent. We envision a future where 100% network uptime is standard, where latency is non-existent, and where every household has access to Gigabit speeds without prohibitive pricing.

  • How much time will it take to install?
    Our standard installation typically takes between 1-3 business days after you place an order. Our expert technicians will set up the router and ensure your coverage is seamless before they leave so you don't face any post-installation issues.
  • What is the average internet speed?
    Depending on the package you choose, speeds range from 50 Mbps for basic home internet up to 1 Gbps (1000 Mbps) for our premium corporate and dedicated server connections. All our fibers have low latency.
  • Can I upgrade my plan later?
    Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your customer portal or by calling our 24/7 support line. Plan changes take effect within 24 hours of confirmation.
  • Do you provide a WiFi router?
    Yes, all of our residential and corporate plans include a state-of-the-art dual-band WiFi router at no additional rental cost. Our technicians will configure the router to ensure maximum coverage across your premises.
